ABOUT

I'm Abraham, a sophomore at Stanford pursuing dual degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science. My research interests center on reinforcement learning and post-training methods for large language models.

I grew up in Hong Kong and attended Eton College in the UK as a King's Scholar, where I graduated as valedictorian. Outside of academics, I sing tenor with the Mendicants (Stanford's oldest all-male a cappella group), serve as a director at BASES Entrepreneurship, and am a member of Phi Kappa Psi.

I care deeply about environmentalism and mentorship. I founded the Climate Changemakers Course, which has reached students in over 15 countries, and served as a Youth Delegate at COP27.
